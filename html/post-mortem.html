Project Expectations

Since the first mention of this project we all had a clear sentence in mind: “We want to do a small game, small and well done, a game we can feel ours proudly”

We are proud of

We are proud of successfully creating a quality small game, despite all the obstacles faced. Also being able to dedicate ourselves fully into a project and giving our best doing so has been an experience that helped everyone grow and further develop their skills.

We need to improve

Communication, having clear workflows, and actively contributing to those aspects, is something that as students we figured out on the way rather than from the start. That is the main improvement we see for our future projects.

Conclusions

First of all, we started a voluntary pre-production stage in summer 2025 since we wanted to create and present our own custom IP to our university so that we created extra motivation for this project and a higher commitment to it. After being successful on doing so, our expectations and volition to do the project skyrocketed making the development a smooth process with high commitment and an incredible early start. This set up made us realize how truly important it is working with committed members with a clear objective.
