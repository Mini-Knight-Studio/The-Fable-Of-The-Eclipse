We are Mini Knight Studios, a team of 19 students from the CITM university in Barcelona, specifically a team of 8 developers, 5 designers, 5 artists, and 1 producer and altogether we decided to create Fable of the Eclipse, a fantasy action-adventure game, based on our own narrative IP and engine.

Fable of the Eclipse is an action-adventure game that follows Brightwiskers, a clever and quick solarian jerboa, who will have to venture into the huge forest and find the elemental gems to save his kind from the god of nature.
