Organization

Guillem Martínez Aparicio

Producer

What started with my volition to take on the role of director for this project, ended up with me doing a wide range of roles(Producer, Director and Programmer) due to the needs of the project, our small team, and the inexperience from being a student. Even though it has led to a huge improvement in all my skills in those wide-ranging of areas, I ended up realizing the value and comfort of having a defined role to work on. Still, I would repeat my efforts, since nothing beats the fact of working on what I love.

Production

In this area, I was in charge of the entire project management, including the maintenance of the organizational tools we used, such as Hack&Plan, Google Drive , Discord, and a bit of the merges and branch assignment in our GitHub repository. Also, I tried to implement many organizational methodologies, such as Scrum teams, evaluation of the team on each milestone , milestone preparation, personal performance reports , group retrospectives , weekly meetings , daily standups , priority task lists , roles and responsibilities documentation , feedback boards , feedback surveys, and so on.

Even trying all those in small doses and showing them in ways easy to access, truth be told, only half of them actually worked in our academic and small team environment.

Realizing this made me approach everything with a different perspective , focusing on the essentials of the role that worked in our environment to keep everything in motion . Instead of trying any complex structures , I adopted a role in which I was the core point of connection inside the project, someone to reach out to easily in case of any arising doubt regarding organization, information about the state of development, or who to communicate with for specific topics or problems.

Programming

Ana Alcázar Cobo

Programmer

Even not being my first project in a group it was a learningfull experience. Teammates full of passion that worked up in what ended in a really great game.

Engine Project Building System

I was in charge of creating the system for building projects inside the custom engine.

Blobs

I was in charge of the elemental blobs, these blobs bites the player, splits into smaller ones when defeated and elemental blobs have effects. Fire blob has a chance of double damage. Water blob has a chance of apply slowness to player.

Golem

I was in charge of the golem, this golem is protecting with its shield until attacking that will be vulnerable and player can attack in order to defeat him.

Boss

I was in charge of the boss called God of Nature, this boss consist in 2 hands throwing patterns of attacks the player needs to evade, burn both hands and head, repeat the pattern and finally burn it to defeat it.

Web

I was in charge of doing this website of the game, with pages showing the project, its process, the studio and team performance, and tools used meanwhile.
