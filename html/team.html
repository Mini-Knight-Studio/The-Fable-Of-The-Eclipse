Art

Paula Laguna López

Artist

Rigging the player is one of the most excruciatingly painful things I have done up to date, which has forced me to learn about Maya much more than I wanted for this project.

Player Animation

I was in charge of doing animations for the player.

Player Rig

I was in charge of rigging the player model. Features FK spine, spring IK legs, IK/FK arms, IK/FK tail, minimal facial rig, prop handles for tail and hand, spine twist control, 2D VFX handles.

Blob Animation

I was in charge of doing animations for the blob enemy.

Blob Rig

I was in charge of rigging the blob model. Features squash and stretch and squash spine, surface constraints for cartoonish eyes and teeth, FK limbs and spine controls and 2D VFX handles and mesh.

Golem Animation

I was in charge of doing animations for the golem enemy.

Golem Rig

I was in charge of rigging the Golem model. Features IK/FK arms, on/off and attached/detached shield toggles, FK legs and 2D VFX handles.

Boss Rig

I was in charge of doing the rig for the boss. Features FK spine, free FK hand rig, IK stretch and squash splines for branches grow motion and simple head control.

Game music

I was in charge of scoring and producing all the game music tracks and tunes.
